    • FOR VISITOR APP

    How your company track visitors? How do you manage visitor's equipment? How do you make sure to manage visitors in secue? How do you reduce paper works for Infomation Desk? How your visitors feel comfortable when visiting your company? etc... Now, with ForVisitor application can resolve all your concerns.

    Visitors check-in

    • Enterprise apps: Information Desk, Kios and Security Guard
    • Visitor app: ForVistior
    • Visitor app automatically detects Information Desk service of company OR scan QR code (Bluetooth-LE base service coming) from Kios for check-in process
    • Install profile and restriction profiles
    • Now your devices are managed by Information Desk and check-in process is completed

    Visitors check-out

    • Visitor app automatically detects Information Desk service of company OR scan QR code (Bluetooth-LE base service coming) from Kios for check-out process
    • Visitors validated automatically
    • Check-out profile is installed
    • Check-out process completed

    Features

    • Visitor registration: Register visitor information to Infomation Desk before checking in
    • Enable/Disable camera: Information Desk can enable/disable camera on visitor's devices
    • Enable/Disable photostream: Information Desk can enable/disable photostream on visitor's devices
    • Enable/Disable youtube: Information Desk can enable/disable youtube on visitor's devices
    • Enable/Disable voice dialing: Information Desk can enable/disable voice dialing on visitor's devices
    • Enable/Disable itune: Information Desk can enable/disable itune on visitor's devices
    • Enable/Disable inapp-purchase: Information Desk can enable/disable inapp-purchase on visitor's devices
    • Guest Wi-Fi configuration settings: all Wi-Fi configurations pushed from Infomation Desk to Visitors
    • iBeacon
    • Privacy policy settings
    • Voice call with your host and information desk
